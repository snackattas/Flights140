Flights140 tracks Twitter for airfare deals, error fares, and flash flares using a special algorithm so you will be instaneously alerted when the flight deals you care about are tweeted!


How it works:
  1. Enter origins and/or destinations.
  2. Receive an alert (via email and/or text) when there is a tweet containing your criteria!
  3. Buy your tickets (before someone else does) and travel for cheap!

This site is designed to find you deals for those far-off destinations where you would only travel to if you found the perfect deal!
