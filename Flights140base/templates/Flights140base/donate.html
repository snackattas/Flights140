Flights140 is free to use, but there are costs to maintaining it. Here are some of the costs:

  • Keeping the domain name
  • Heroku server uptime
  • Twilio for notifying you via text
  • Sendgrid for notifying you via email
  • Time spent creating and maintaining the website

Donations are greatly appreciated. Recommended donation is $3, the cost of a cup of coffee. If you have found a deal using Flights140, donating would be a great way of saying thank you!
