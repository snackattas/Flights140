Flights140 is free to use, but there are costs to maintaining it. Here are some of the costs:

  • Keeping the domain name
  • Heroku server uptime
  • Twilio for texts
  • Sendgrid for emails
  • Time spent creating and maintaining the website

Donations are greatly appreciated. Recommended donation is $3, the cost of a cup o' joe these days. If you've found a deal using Flights140, donating would be a great way of saying thanks!
